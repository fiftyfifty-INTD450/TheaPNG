August 23rd, 2003

I don’t really know who I’m writing this for. I can’t tell my family, my friends, or my classmates about this, they wouldn’t believe me anyway. Fuck, I can barely bring myself to even think about it, let alone write down in my diary on our computer.

I really thought that I could make this work. I thought that I could have a great relationship with Sam, just the two of us falling in love and making the best of it. After Dakota, I wanted to be a better partner and I didn’t want it to break away like it did with them. But now, I feel like I can’t do anything, and it’s slowly suffocating me.

My parents can’t help me. They have no money. They have to pay off their mortgage and now my student loans. I can’t disappoint them again.
